Privacy Policy

Omnilh is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information, and our commitment to user privacy.

Information Collection

We operate a website primarily for informational purposes, and we do not actively collect any personal information from our users. When you browse our website, we do not track your personal data nor do we ask you to provide personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your preferences. These cookies do not contain any personal data and are solely used to improve your browsing experience. Please note that you can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

While we do not actively collect personal information, please be aware that our website may include links to third-party services, such as social media platforms or mapping services. These third parties may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review their privacy statements before engaging with their services.

Transparency and Accountability

We are committed to transparency in our privacy practices. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us at: info@omnilh.com

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of how we protect your privacy.
